游戏操作指南 (中文)

  • 移动：W A S D 键
  • 攻击：鼠标瞄准 + 左键射击
  • 目标：尽可能长时间地生存下去

How to Play (English)

  • Movement: W A S D keys
  • Attack: Aim with mouse + Left-click
  • Objective: Survive as long as possible
  • Pickups: Collect ground items for buffs and debuff
